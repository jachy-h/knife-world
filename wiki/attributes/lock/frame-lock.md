# 框架锁

## 中文
刀柄框架本身切出弹性锁片。开刀后锁片横向弹入刀根斜面，以最少零件形成直接、坚固的承力路径。

## English
A spring section cut directly from the handle frame moves behind the blade tang, creating a direct and robust load path with very few parts.

# 直刀结构

## 中文
刀身与刀柄之间没有折叠关节，也就不需要锁定机构。连续刀茎直接把载荷传入刀柄，结构最为直接。

## English
With no folding joint, no lock is required. A continuous tang carries load directly into the handle—the simplest structural path.

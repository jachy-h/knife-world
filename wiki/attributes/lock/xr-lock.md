# XR 横轴锁

## 中文
双侧可操作的横轴锁栓在刀根后方承力。拉回锁栓释放刀刃，兼顾左右手操作与手指避让。

## English
An ambidextrous crossbar bears behind the blade tang. Pulling it back releases the blade while keeping fingers outside the closing path.

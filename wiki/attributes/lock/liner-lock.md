# 衬片锁

## 中文
刀柄内部的弹性钢衬横向顶住刀根斜面。推开钢衬即可收刀，结构轻薄且适合单手操作。

## English
A spring liner inside the handle moves laterally against the tang ramp. Pushing it aside releases the blade; the construction is slim and one-hand friendly.

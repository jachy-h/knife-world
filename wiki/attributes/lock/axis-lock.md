# 横轴锁

## 中文
弹簧推动横向锁栓卡入刀根斜面。锁栓横跨两侧钢衬，受力较均衡；向后拉动锁栓即可释放，手指无需进入刃口路径。

## English
Springs drive a transverse bar against a ramp on the blade tang. The bar spans both liners for balanced lockup; pulling it back releases the blade while fingers stay clear of the edge path.

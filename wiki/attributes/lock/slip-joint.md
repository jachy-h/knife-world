# 滑动关节

## 中文
背部弹簧压住刀根平面，只提供定位阻力而不形成真正锁定。它结构传统，但使用时不能依赖锁止承载。

## English
A backspring presses against flats on the tang to provide positional resistance, not a true lock. It is traditional and should not be relied on for lock-bearing loads.

# Tri-Ad 三点锁

## 中文
在传统背锁基础上增加独立止刀柱，将刀根载荷分散到柄架；锁杆与刀根的几何还能随磨损继续深入咬合。

## English
An independent stop pin redistributes tang loads into the frame. The rocker geometry can seat progressively deeper as components wear.

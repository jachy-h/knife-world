# 弧轨锁

## 中文
控制钮沿弧形轨道运动，同时驱动内部机构释放刀根。开合路径被直接转化为外部可见的圆周动作。

## English
A control travels through an arced track to release the tang, translating the internal deployment path into a visible circular gesture.

# 鲨鱼锁

## 中文
刀背上的弹簧锁块从上方咬合刀根。向后拉动外露锁鳍即可解锁，闭合时手指不必经过刃口路径。

## English
A spring-loaded block above the tang locks the blade. Pulling the exposed fin rearward releases it, allowing closure away from the edge path.

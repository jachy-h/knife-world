# 按钮锁

## 中文
横向柱塞在弹簧作用下进入刀根缺口。按下按钮让柱塞退出缺口，从而释放刀刃。

## English
A spring-loaded transverse plunger enters a recess in the tang. Pressing the button withdraws the plunger and releases the blade.
